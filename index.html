Since 1998

Junior Tours - A Smarter Approach to Student Travel started in 1998 with one yellow school bus and one destination: an eighth-grade trip to Washington, D.C. We still run that trip every spring. Since then we've added New York City history tours, Orlando theme park weekends, and custom itineraries built around whatever a teacher's curriculum calls for.

What hasn't changed is how we work. One trip coordinator handles your group from the first phone call to the last mile home. We've toured the hotels ourselves and eaten at the restaurants before we ever put them on an itinerary. When a bus needs a new alternator outside of Hagerstown at eleven at night, and it has happened, our coordinator is already on the phone with a mechanic before the driver finishes the call to us.
