What happens after you reach out

01

We listen first

A trip coordinator calls to talk through your goals: the subject the trip supports, your budget per student, and who's coming along.

02

We build the itinerary

Within a few days you'll have a day-by-day plan with lodging, transportation, and activities already priced out.

03

You review and adjust

We revise until it fits your calendar and your school's approval process, then lock in dates and deposit.
